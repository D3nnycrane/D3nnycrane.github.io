About Me

What inspired me to work in cybersecurity?

As a young man, the 90's stylised hacking was always super cool to me. As an adult, the idea that I am keeping regular people safe from the bad ones is animating.

What project am I most proud of?

My first ever hacking write-up. From someone who had no qualifications, to a published CTF write-up, made me know I could do it if I tried.

What’s the most valuable lesson I learned in my first tech role?

Time in the job, and experience, are not the same thing. Don't do the same thing for three years, do something different every day.

What types of security problems do I naturally gravitate toward solving?

Prioritisation. There is always a fire to put out somewhere, but knowing which one can wait, and which could burn us down in minutes, is an animating challenge.
